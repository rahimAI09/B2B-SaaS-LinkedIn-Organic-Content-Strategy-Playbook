## Post 2: 8 Things for Positioning
**Date:** May 2026
**Content:**
8 things worth considering if you're working on positioning:
1. It's not about being "better," it's about being "different."
2. Define the category before the features.
3. Your homepage is a sales pitch, not a library.
4. Focus on the "status quo" as your biggest competitor.
5. Good positioning makes the price irrelevant.
6. Use customer language, not marketing jargon.
7. Positioning is a sprint, not a marathon.
8. Test your messaging on real people, not your team.

## Post 3: The Status Quo Competitor
**Date:** June 2025
**Content:**
Most B2B SaaS founders think their competitor is another SaaS. 
It's not. 
It's the spreadsheet they've used for 10 years. 
It's the "way we've always done it." 
Position against the pain of the status quo.
