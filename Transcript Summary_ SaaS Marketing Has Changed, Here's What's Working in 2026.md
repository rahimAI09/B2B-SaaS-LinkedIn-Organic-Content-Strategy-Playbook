# Transcript Summary: SaaS Marketing Has Changed, Here's What's Working in 2026
**Source:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Ks0KnzB0dtg
**Speaker:** Sam Dunning
**Key Insights:**
- The "deadly trio" for 2026: SEO/AEO, LinkedIn, and YouTube.
- SEO is no longer just about ranking on Google; it's about being the answer in AI search (Answer Engine Optimization).
- YouTube is a trust-building machine for B2B. Long-form video builds more trust than a 500-word blog post.
- LinkedIn should be used to amplify the insights found in your YouTube videos and SEO content.
- Focus on "Revenue SEO"—targeting keywords that indicate a high intent to purchase.
