# Chris Walker LinkedIn Posts

## Post 1: Creating vs. Capturing Demand
**Date:** ~2023/2024 (Classic Framework)
**Content:**
Most companies only focus on capturing existing demand. They wait for people to look for them.
Creating demand happens where people hang out (LinkedIn, Podcasts). You educate them on the problem before they even know they need a solution.
LinkedIn isn't for leads; it's for demand.

## Post 2: The "Dark Social" Reality
**Date:** 2025/2026
**Content:**
Your attribution software is lying to you. 
The biggest deals happen in "Dark Social"—LinkedIn posts, Slack groups, and private DMs. 
If you stop posting on LinkedIn because your CRM says it doesn't "convert," you're killing your pipeline at the source.
