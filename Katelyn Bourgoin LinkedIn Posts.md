## Post 1: Buyer Psychology is the Growth Hack
**Date:** September 2025
**Content:**
Buyer psychology is the real growth hack. 
Transformation > Features. 
Your customers don't care about your "AI-powered dashboard." 
They care about not getting fired. 
They care about getting home in time for dinner. 
Market to the human, not the title.
