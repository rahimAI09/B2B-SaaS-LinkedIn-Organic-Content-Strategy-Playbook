# B2B SaaS LinkedIn Strategy Frameworks

## 1. The "Founder Brand" Framework (Dave Gerhardt)
- **Identify the Voice:** Who is the face of the company?
- **Content Pillars:** What 3 topics will they own?
- **Consistency:** Post at least 3x per week.
- **Engagement:** Spend 15 minutes a day replying to comments.

## 2. The "Dark Social" Demand Gen Model (Chris Walker)
- **Create Value:** Share insights, not ads.
- **Distribution:** Post where the buyers are (LinkedIn, Slack, Podcasts).
- **Measurement:** Use "How did you hear about us?" fields on demo forms.

## 3. The "Create Once, Distribute Forever" (Ross Simmonds)
- **Core Asset:** 1 Video or 1 Whitepaper.
- **Micro-Assets:** 10 LinkedIn posts, 5 Tweets, 1 Newsletter, 3 Short-form videos.
- **Loop:** Use feedback from LinkedIn to improve the core asset.

## 4. The Solopreneur Content Engine (Justin Welsh)
- **Hook:** Grab attention in the first line.
- **Story:** Provide a relatable narrative or data.
- **Offer:** A soft call to action (Newsletter sign-up or comment).
- **Repurpose:** Use a "Content Matrix" to turn 1 idea into 4 different formats.
