# B2B SaaS LinkedIn Organic Content Strategy Research

This repository contains a curated collection of research materials focused on LinkedIn organic content strategies specifically for B2B SaaS.

## Why LinkedIn Organic for B2B SaaS?
LinkedIn remains the primary platform for B2B decision-makers. While paid ads are effective, organic content builds long-term authority, trust, and "dark social" demand that is harder to replicate. This research project dives into the playbooks of 10 experts who have mastered this engine.

## Research Structure
- `/research/sources.md`: List of experts with links and annotations.
- `/research/linkedin-posts/`: Recent high-performing posts from the selected experts.
- `/research/youtube-transcripts/`: Transcripts from relevant video content (strategy deep-dives).
- `/research/other/`: Newsletters, articles, and additional frameworks.
