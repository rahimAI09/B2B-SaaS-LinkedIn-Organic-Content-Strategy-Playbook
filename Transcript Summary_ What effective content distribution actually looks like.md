# Transcript Summary: What effective content distribution actually looks like
**Source:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=TpmBInAnY3Y
**Speaker:** Ross Simmonds
**Key Insights:**
- Most B2B brands suffer from a "publish and pray" mentality.
- Distribution is the act of getting your content in front of your ICP where they already are.
- The "Create Once, Distribute Everywhere" (CODE) framework involves breaking down one long-form asset (like a whitepaper) into 50+ micro-assets.
- Use communities like Reddit and Quora to find the questions people are asking, then answer them on LinkedIn.
- Repurpose successful LinkedIn posts into Twitter threads, and vice versa.
