# Ross Simmonds LinkedIn Posts

## Post 1: Content Distribution vs. Publishing
**Date:** ~July 2025
**Content:**
Most brands publish content. But they should be distributing insight. 
Publishing says: "We just released this 20 page PDF. Go read it."
Distribution says: "Here are the 3 most important insights from our new 20-page PDF."
Don't just publish. Distribute.

## Post 2: Six Trends for LinkedIn in 2026
**Date:** November 2025
**Content:**
What should brands & creators do on LinkedIn to win in 2026? Here's six trends:
1) More raw, real & unpolished content.
2) The rise of the "Founder Brand".
3) Niche communities over broad reach.
4) Video-first strategy for authority.
5) AI-assisted clarity, not just efficiency.
6) Repurposing long-form into micro-insights.

## Post 3: The "Dark Social" Pipeline
**Date:** April 2026
**Content:**
Distribution, Not Content, Was the Problem. 
Every marketer has had this moment: You spend 20 hours on a post. You share it once. 6 months later, it has 200 views and zero pipeline impact.
The solution? Reverse engineer top-performing formats from communities like Reddit and repurpose them for LinkedIn.

## Post 4: Video as the Most Reputable Asset
**Date:** June 2026
**Content:**
What's the best content format for B2B brands? Video. 
It's the most reputable asset you can create. You can take the audio and turn it into a podcast. You can take the transcript and turn it into 5 LinkedIn posts. You can take the insights and turn it into a newsletter.
