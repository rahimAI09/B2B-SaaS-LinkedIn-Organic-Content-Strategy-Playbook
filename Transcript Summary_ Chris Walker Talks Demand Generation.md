# Transcript Summary: Chris Walker Talks Demand Generation
**Source:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=o8OqbkD2jSM
**Speaker:** Chris Walker
**Key Insights:**
- Lead generation is about capturing existing demand. Demand generation is about creating new demand.
- Content on LinkedIn should be educational and provide value without asking for anything in return.
- "Dark Social" is the ecosystem where buyers talk to each other without being tracked by marketing software.
- B2B companies need to shift their focus from MQLs (Marketing Qualified Leads) to HIRO (High Intent Revenue Opportunities).
- The best LinkedIn content is POV-led (Point of View) and challenges the status quo.
