# Dave Gerhardt LinkedIn Posts

## Post 1: The "Founder Brand" Playbook
**Date:** 2025
**Content:**
Marketing kills marketing. 
The best B2B brands sound like people, not robots. 
Use your personal profile to tell the story of why you built the product. 
Don't sell features; sell the transformation.

## Post 2: B2B Copywriting on LinkedIn
**Date:** 2026
**Content:**
Write like you talk. 
Most B2B LinkedIn posts are boring because they try to sound "professional."
Professional = Boring. 
Personal = Engaging. 
Use short sentences. Use line breaks. Talk to one person.

## Post 3: Community as a Moat
**Date:** 2026
**Content:**
Exit Five is a community, not just a list. 
The best LinkedIn strategy is to build a community where your customers talk to each other. 
Your content should spark conversations, not just broadcast information.
